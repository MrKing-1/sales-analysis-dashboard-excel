## 📊 Excel Sales Analysis Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and Slicers to support data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Objectives

-Analyze overall sales performance.

-Identify top-performing product categories.

-Evaluate customer demographics.

-Track monthly customer trends.

-Compare revenue by gender and product category.

-Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

## 🛠️ Tools & Features

-Microsoft Excel

-Pivot Tables

-Pivot Charts

-KPI Cards

-Slicers

-Data Visualization

-Dashboard Design


## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

-Total Customers: 3,900

-Total Revenue: £5,180,929

-Average Customer Age: 52

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

-Total Revenue by Category

-Total Customers by Category

-Monthly Customer Trend

-Revenue by Gender

-Customers by Age Group

-Payment Methods by Age Group

-Revenue Distribution by Year

-Interactive Order Day Slicer

## 🔍 Key Insights

-3,900 customers generated £5.18M in revenue.

-Clothing recorded the highest revenue and customer count.

-Footwear generated the lowest revenue.

-Adults formed the largest customer segment.

-Male customers generated more revenue than females.

-May recorded the highest customer count, while July had the lowest.

### 💡 Recommendations

-Increase investment in the Clothing category to maximize revenue.

-Promote Footwear and Outerwear through targeted campaigns.

-Develop marketing strategies to attract more Young Adult customers.

-Launch promotions during low-performing months to improve sales.

-Introduce loyalty programs for Adult and Senior customers.

-Encourage digital payment methods to enhance customer convenience.
